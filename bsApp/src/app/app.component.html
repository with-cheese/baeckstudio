Welcome to Bäck! A design studio based in Stockholm, Sweden.
 We offer graphic design, branding and art direction for cultural and commercial clients. For inquiries contact info@baeck.studio or visit us at Atlasgatan 14




{{ image.title }}

Founded 2017 by Josefin Gahmberg and Clara Lidström, Bäck specialises in graphic design, branding and art direction. Their work includes publications, exhibition design, brand identities and digital solutions. Josefin and Clara studied at Berghs School of Communication (2011–2013) and have previously worked for agencies such as BankerWessel, Frankenstein Studio, ill Studio, Stockholm Graphics, Vice and MnO. For a meeting or inquires please contact info@baeck.studio
